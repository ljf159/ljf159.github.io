Short Biography

I am a Carnegie Bosch Fellow at Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) with Prof. Pingbo Tang from Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering and Prof. Cleotilde Gonzalez from Department of Social & Decision Sciences. Prior to joing CMU, I was a member of iLab at The University of Hong Kong. Currently, I am doing research on human-AI collaboration for improving urban subway flood responses.

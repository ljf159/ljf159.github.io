About

Short Biography

I am Jinfeng Lou (娄金峰), currently a PhD Candidate at Faculty of Architecture, HKU, as well as a member of iLab. My current research focuses on：
(1) Exploring information management in construction projects with advanced ITs, e.g., blockchain, IoT;
(2) Optimizing the modular construction supply chain under the principle of DfX (Design for Excellence).
